Need a vacation? Choose your own adventure from these 28 weekend getaways

Whether you’re ready to fly your freak flag high on a Star Wars- or Twin Peaks-themed adventure or just seriously need a mind-calming spa day away from the office, we all have our reasons for hitting the road. But regardless of what we’re getting away from — the rain (too gloomy), the sun (too hot!), the pull of social media and our phones (too addictive!), or even an excess of travel (too much logistics-wrangling!) — most of us have one thing in common when we finally resolve to use those vacation days or book that trip over Labor Day: the reality that our strictly recreational time and resources available to spend on it are limited.

But don’t despair: We’ve created a choose-your-own adventure collection of tried-and-tested weekend getaways to locales as far-flung as a luge run in the Methow Valley and as close by as an opulent hotel in downtown Seattle, as esoteric as a resort on the coast specializing in old-school travel-trailers and as ordinary as summer camp. We actually tested these trips out, with an emphasis on much more than the Instagram experience, so what you won’t find here is a vacation that looks good on social media but doesn’t fit your needs. Instead, you’ll find something better: a realistic itinerary you can likely complete in a weekend, tailored to your exact reasons for wanting to get out of Dodge.

Our vacation time is limited. Make it count.
