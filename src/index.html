Need a vacation? Choose your own adventure from these 28 weekend getaways

Sick of routine and want a new adventure? Need a mind-calming spa day away from the office? Sometimes, everyone needs a quick getaway to refresh and rejuvenate. And most of us have one thing in common: vacation time and the resources to spend on it are limited commodities. So we’ve created a choose-your-own-adventure collection of tried-and-tested weekend getaways to a variety of locales. Just tell us what you’re hoping to get away from, then select from our menu of options and get going! Ready?
